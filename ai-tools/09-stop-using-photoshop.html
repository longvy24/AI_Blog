Three Things To Correct Upfront

Not once. No name, no logo, no URL, no interface shot clear enough to identify the product. On top of that, two specific claims in the script about the free plan are inaccurate based on the sourced Clipdrop review used for this post.

The Tool

What Clipdrop Actually Does

Clipdrop is an AI image editing suite built for speed. Originally developed by Init.ai and acquired by Stability AI in 2022, it's now owned by Jasper (the AI writing company) following their acquisition in 2023. The core product hasn't changed much through the ownership shifts — it's still a collection of fast, browser-based photo editing tools that require zero design skill.

The video demonstrates three features: background removal (one click, clean cutout), object removal ("Remove Object" button — select what you don't want, it fills in what was behind it), and background generation (type a prompt like "sunset beach," click Generate, get a photorealistic scene). All three are available on the free tier with daily limits. All three work as described.

What the video doesn't show: Clipdrop also does image upscaling (up to 16x), image relighting (change the light source in a photo), text removal, uncropping (expand a photo beyond its original edges), and universal resizing for social media formats. The API is available for developers who want to automate image processing pipelines. It's a deeper toolset than the 50-second video suggests.

The Three Demos

Each Feature Verified

  • Background Removal — Upload a photo, one click, background gone. Works on hair edges and complex subjects. Free tier: 20 uses/day. Result: clean PNG with transparent background. This is Clipdrop's most-used feature and it genuinely does what the script says.
  • Object / Person Removal — Select the thing you don't want (a person, an object, a sign), click Remove Object, Clipdrop fills in the background behind it using AI inpainting. Works best on simple backgrounds. Complex backgrounds sometimes leave artifacts. Verified accurate as demonstrated.
  • Background Generation — Type a prompt ("sunset beach," "office," "studio white"), Clipdrop removes the existing background and generates a photorealistic replacement. This is the most impressive demo in the video and it's real. Quality varies by prompt complexity — simple scenes work better than elaborate ones.
